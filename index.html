about me

Clifford is unique

I've been working with digital media in some form for over 10 years, and I love learning new things everyday. My enthusiasm for technology is the human factor, the user interface can be known as the keystone to any good project. With a background in design and engineering, I think with an artistic and scientific mind
- bringing a unique perspective to the table.

Clifford the Sailor

It all started after high-school with a tour in the Navy where I studied electronics engineering. While gaining experience with maintaining and troubleshooting complex electronic and electromechanical systems, I also learned a lot of other key values during this period.

After an honorable discharge from the Navy I joined a Department of Defense Contractor where I got to use my talents to make the Navy's ship entertainment systems a little better. During my tenure with the Defense Media Activity I was the lead engineer in prototyping a next generation broadcast studio system for larger size Navy ships.

Clifford is Creative

While I was prototyping the Navy's SITE 400 TV system I gained an appreciation for how users interact with systems. This is where my creative confidence and interest in human-computer interaction really started to grow.

I completed that project and went back to school to study User Interface Design. This pursuit eventually lead me to San Diego's Platt College where I graduated with a 4.0 gpa as Valedictorian for my class with a Bachelors Degree in Web Design.
